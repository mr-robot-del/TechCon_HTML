History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into an international conference, hosting over 5,000 attendees annually. From its humble beginnings focused on software development, it now covers AI, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the technology sector. We aim to bridge the gap between industry leaders and aspiring professionals, promoting ethical tech practices and diversity in STEM fields.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has featured some of the brightest minds in tech. Here are a few highlights:

  • Elon Musk (2022): Discussed sustainable energy and space exploration's role in humanity's future.
  • Satya Nadella (2021): Shared insights on cloud computing and AI ethics.
  • Reshma Saujani (2020): Advocated for women in tech and coding education.
